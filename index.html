Telnet BBS Access (WE6JBO)

The WE6JBO BBS is an experimental bulletin board system accessible over Telnet. It is designed for hobbyists, retro-computing fans, and anyone interested in classic BBS-style communication and amateur radio culture.

Connect via Telnet from a terminal or BBS client: 

telnet kg09e62.glddns.com 23

If your system supports Telnet URLs, you can also try: telnet://kg09e62.glddns.com:23

Note: Telnet is an older, unencrypted protocol. For security, connect only from systems you control and understand.
